Cloth Simulation Demos

Educational cloth simulation using a shared physics core (cloth.js) with three different rendering backends. The cloth starts horizontal, one edge pinned, gravity enabled, with mouse grab, drag, and wind interaction.

  • three.js Renderer
  • Canvas 2D Renderer
  • Raw WebGL Renderer
Controls:
• Click / drag: grab cloth
• Shift + drag: apply wind
• W: toggle wind
